Facciamo presto —
A digital agency of the modern ages

We are starting a digital agency.
In 2015.
What it means to be a digital agency in 2015?
When we begun, the web (and the world) was quite different.

Before of the smartphone mass explosion occurred, we probably wouldn't have ever thinked to make a website usable through something different than a personal computer.

Now things are a little bit changed. Billions of people are using the web for a lot of things in their life.

It means that you — as an agency — are no longer responsible only for the "implementation details", like how you make a logo or what technology you adopt.

You are now in charge to make the web an accessible and democratic place.
For everyone. It's a responsibility.

It means that you have to put a lot of efforts and attention in order to understand the cultural background and the behaviours of the users, before talking of "web design".

Spend a lot of time to learn how they use an app or a website. Why they need it. And what do they aim to do with it.

It means your role is simply changed and it involves a lot more to deal back with human behaviours rather than machines.

At the end, who are we building things for?
